Developing nation-wide health trend management to make informed health policy decisions

There exist significant challenges in communication, coordination, and management of health information in developing nations such as Tanzania, Ethiopia, and Kenya.

We track nation-wide health data in real-time and create actionable statistics.

Non-Profit support will be able to measure the impact of their aid with comprehensive metrics, governments will be able to make informed decisions on health policies, and medical suppliers will be able to predict the current and future demand. Most importantly, Medicron will allow the citizens to have a more effective and scientific approach to the way health care treatment and management is done.
